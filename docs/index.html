Regal's Control Solutions

The Benshaw® brand provides low voltage solid state starters and AC drives for a wide range of applications from pumps, compressors, fans, and blowers. We have earned a solid reputation for building rugged, reliable performance. User friendly features include on-boarding metering and user-selectable options for all of our control products.

Solid State Starters

Benshaw® builds standard and engineered starter products that include a range of open chassis with continuous duty non-bypassed starters, open chassis starters with integral bypass, and prepackaged reduced voltage solid state starters. Combination circuit breaker configurations, with non-bypassed continuous duty configurations and integral bypass configurations, are available to suit your application requirements.

All of Benshaw®’s low voltage stacks can be integrated with Benshaw®’s MX2 or next generation MX3 control technology.

AC Drives

Benshaw®’s drive products include an intuitive user interface, integrated control and power electronics, and can be provided with bypass and isolation contactors. Additionally, we offer a host of options that tailor our AC drives to the specific requirements of the application. Our engineer and design teams draw on experience of extensive real-world drive applications. If a standard drive doesn’t fit your needs, Benshaw® can engineer and build one that does— with the shortest lead time in the industry.

Regal's Pump Solutions

The Marathon brand’s of pump motors are at the heart of the pump to provide reliable, dependable service in the most demanding applications and the harshest environments.

Pump Motors

Regal is an industry leader in low and medium voltage NEMA and IEC motors for the water and wastewater industry. The low voltage motor efficiency levels include IE2, IE3 and IE4.

The low voltage products include: Close-Coupled JM/JP/C-Face motors in open and TEFC construction, Footed and Footless; Vertical Solid Shaft P-Base motors in open and TEFC construction; Vertical Hollow Shaft motors in WPI and WPII construction; Severe Duty motors in TEFC construction; PMAC Severe Duty motors in TEFC construction.

The medium voltage motors are built in NEMA and IEC configurations and include: NEMA open (IP23) and TEFC construction; IEC open, Weather-Protected I, Weather-Protected II, TEFC, TEACC, and TEWAC construction. These motors are built in high efficiency designs.

The voltage range is from 2300V to 13.8kV 50HZ and 60HZ.

All of Marathon motors meet or exceed NEMA and IEC requirements. They are CE Certified, CSA Certified, NOM and UL Listed as standard.

Regal's Fan Solutions

Both the Genteq® and Marathon® brands of fan motors have been industry leaders for over 100 years: producing single and three phase induction motors and leading-edge BLDC technologies.

Induction Motors

Marathon Motors® produces a broad array of single and three phase NEMA and IEC induction motors for a wide range of fan and blower applications. Marathon® builds open, TEFC and explosion proof fan and blower motors in 48 and 56 frames.

BLDC Motors

Genteq® has led the fan and blower industry for over 30 years with innovative products such as the first generation of electrically commutated motors which were introduced in 1987 and used in residential HVAC systems. Today, Genteq® incorporates their patented BLDC technology to a wide range of applications in commercial and industrial fans. These high-performance motors offer exceptional energy savings, performance, system communications, direct drop-in for induction motors and ease of installation.

All of Genteq® and Marathon Motors® meet NEMA and IEC requirements. They are CE Certified, CSA Certified and UL Listed.

Regal's Generator Solutions

For over 50 years, the Marathon® generator brand has provided solutions for pump houses and lift stations in critical applications within the water and wastewater industry. Regal has built a reputation for providing powerful performance, reliable power generation, and easy installation.

MAGNAPLUS®

The MAGNAPLUS® generator range is from 5 to 430kW 60HZ and from 5 to 420kW 50HZ. The MAGNAPLUS® is a ruggedly constructed, field adaptable, permanent magnet generator. For installations requiring fault current support or applications involving non-linear loads, it can be coupled with our innovative PM300 or DVR2000E voltage regulator. Additionally, the MAGNAPLUS® generators are fully guarded for operator safety and generator protection.

MAGNAMAXDVR®

The MAGNAMAXDVR® generator has a range of 118 to 2370kW 60HZ and from 136 to 2400kW 50 HZ. It is the ideal choice for larger loads and offers exceptional transient performance. The DVR2000E digital voltage regulator provides 25% regulation and three phase RMS sensing. The generator’s heavy duty construction, oversized conduit box, Class H insulation system, and shielded bearing system gives years of trouble-free service in the toughest of environments.

All Marathon® generators meet NEMA MG1-32, BS5000, and IEC 34-1 requirements. They are CE Certified, CSA Certified, and UL Listed.
